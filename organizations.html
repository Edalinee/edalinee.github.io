ORGANIZATIONS


National Junior Honor Society

In my 8th grade school year (2012-2013) I was part of an organization called National Junior Honor Society, or NJHS. NJHS was a front for the honor students to come together and serve the community. I was elected President of this organization and was in charge of being a student leader and mentor for my peers in the group.

National Honor Society

Moving on from the middle school organization, in my sophomore year I was able to apply to be part of National Honor Society. This organization requires a total of 10 hours of community service for each semester and 20 hours for the summer months. My first semester of being in the club I was able to exceed the limit with 21 community service hours.

National Math Honor Society (Mu Alpha Theta)

National Math Honor Society is a branch from the Honor Society Tree which also entails a plethora of volunteer hours. Being directed towards students who love math, I found this club as a perfect fit.

CLAW

CLAW is an organization aimed at helping the upcoming freshman in our school adjust to high school life. My experiences being taught important values as a freshman inspired me to become a member of this club when I became a sophomore. CLAW teaches attributes like the 7 Habits of Highly Effective Teens.(Be Proactive, Begin with the End in Mind, Put First Things First, Seek First to Understand, Think Win-Win, Synergize, Sharpen the Saw)

Hawkathon

Hawkathon or previously known as Dance Marathon is an Organization started by my friends and I to help raise money for sick kids at Children’s Hospital. This organization is practiced in colleges as well and our common motto is “For the Kids (FTK)”, but “Hawkathon” is unique to our school. I am our Treasurer but I also handle a deal of social relations like promotion of our fundraisers and obtaining sponsors

Buisness Professionals of America

Business Professionals of America or BPA is an organization that encompasses many different topics for a student to learn and compete in. My topic was C++.Unfortunately after learning all year, events inspired me not to be able to compete. Nonetheless I served as the club’s Secretary.

Clubs in Motion

In my Junior Year I will be enrolling in my school’s Model UN which will teach me more about our growing world and the art of debating and speech making. I also enrolled as a founding member of our Programming Club, which is a fairly new addition and in the process of taking off.
